About Great Talk Africa

Your trusted partner for premium outsourcing solutions from Africa

Our Story

Great Talk Africa was founded with a simple mission: to connect businesses worldwide with Africa's exceptional talent pool. Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, we specialize in providing premium call center, customer support, virtual assistant, and executive assistant services.

We believe that Africa offers some of the most dedicated, skilled, and customer-focused professionals in the world. Our team is committed to delivering excellence, reliability, and scalability – helping businesses grow without the headaches of building in-house teams.

From startups to established enterprises, we partner with organizations across industries to provide cost-effective, high-quality outsourcing solutions.
